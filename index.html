Who I Am

I'm a student at the Air Force Institute of Technology, Kaduna, building things that solve real problems for the people around me — not just portfolio filler. My latest project, a full-featured exam simulator for AFIT's Post-UTME candidates, came out of watching students struggle with prep tools that didn't exist yet, so I built one.

Outside of shipping Django apps, I spend time in a home lab running Kali Linux against Metasploitable, learning how systems break so I can understand how to defend them. I'm currently working toward a structured path through Security+, CySA+, and tools like Wazuh and Wireshark — while keeping one foot in full-stack development.

I'd rather build something rough that works than something polished that doesn't ship.
